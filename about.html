About FurrowFlow

Our Story

FurrowFlow was founded in 2020 by Itumeleng Sematle, a passionate agronomist from Maseru who saw the need for quality, affordable agricultural supplies tailored to Lesotho's unique climate and soil conditions.

What started as a small seed exchange among local farmers has grown into a trusted supplier serving over 1,500 farmers across all 10 districts of Lesotho. Our name, FurrowFlow, reflects our commitment to the life-giving flow of water through irrigation furrows that sustain Lesotho's agriculture.

Today, we offer over 200 products including seeds, tools, fertilizers, and irrigation systems – all carefully selected for local conditions. We're proud to support Lesotho's farmers from smallholders to commercial operations.

Our Values

  • Quality First: Every product is tested for Lesotho conditions
  • Sustainable Practices: Promoting water conservation and organic methods
  • Farmer Education: Free growing guides and workshops
  • Local Support: Based in Maseru with local delivery and pickup
  • Fair Pricing: Making quality inputs accessible to all farmers
